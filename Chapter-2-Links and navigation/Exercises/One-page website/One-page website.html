Marvel Studios Loki

Plot overview

The story is about Loki escaping the events of Avenger:Endgame into another timeline, in which Loki was then captured by the TVA(Time Variance Authority). The TVA is an organization that takes place outside time and space, who monitors the proper flow of the sacred timeline. Loki questions his reality of being a god of mischief, as he was introduced into a world wherein everything he believe in was an orchestrated storyline. This series also showcases different variants of loki from different timelines, like Sylvie, (A woman variant of loki) which the TVA is hunting.

Key information

The MCU(Marvel Cinematic Universe) is an interconnected franchise that is based from the characters Marvel comics. Loki first premiered in Disney+ which has 6 episodes. Loki is one of the series that in phase 4 of the MCU that will unveil more about the multiverse sage, which will connect all the events that takes place after Loki. Loki is an integral part of the series as it is the foundation of the idea of the MCU's multiversal concept.
