Marvel Studios Loki

Reviews

The centrality of the romance in Loki makes it stand out within the MCU-and signals how much potential there is in this kind of storytelling, should Marvel choose to mine it. -Alison Herman

Hiddleston's performance as Loki has always been defined by its humour as much as its pathos. Both seem to be on display in equal measure in Loki. -Michael Idato

Without a doubt, the visuals on this show far surpass not only the other Disney+ shows, but actually a lot of Marvel's films as well. The visual effects, cinematography and the set pieces were incredible, I would even say iconic. -Joseph Garcia

Tom Hiddleston once again proves why Loki remains the MCU's standout villain, while also holding together a truly masterful multiverse story that hits upon themes of identity and agency that have always felt core to the character. -Jessie Gender
