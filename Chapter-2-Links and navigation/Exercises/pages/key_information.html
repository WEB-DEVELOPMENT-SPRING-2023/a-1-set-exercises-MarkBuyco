Marvel Studios Loki

Key information

The MCU(Marvel Cinematic Universe) is an interconnected franchise that is based from the characters Marvel comics. Loki first premiered in Disney+ which has 6 episodes. Loki is one of the series that in phase 4 of the MCU that will unveil more about the multiverse sage, which will connect all the events that takes place after Loki. Loki is an integral part of the series s it is the foundation of the idea of the MCU's multiversal concept.
